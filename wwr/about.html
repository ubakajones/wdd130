History

Dry Oar Rafting began in 1995 with a passion for outdoor adventure and a desire to share the excitement of whitewater rafting with others. Over the years, we have guided thousands of guests through breathtaking rivers and unforgettable experiences.

Our mission is to provide safe, exciting, and memorable rafting adventures while helping visitors appreciate the beauty of the natural world.
